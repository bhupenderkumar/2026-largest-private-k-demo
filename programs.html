Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for children aged 3-4, providing a nurturing environment that encourages social, emotional, and cognitive development. Our experienced teachers use play-based learning to introduce basic skills in reading, writing, and math.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students in grades K-4, focusing on core subjects like reading, writing, math, science, and social studies. Our small class sizes and experienced teachers ensure that every student receives personalized attention and support.

📊

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper programs serve students in grades 5-8, emphasizing academic rigor, critical thinking, and character development. Our teachers use a variety of teaching approaches to engage students and prepare them for high school and beyond.

⚗️

STEM

Our STEM program integrates science, technology, engineering, and math to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the world around them. From coding to robotics, our students develop essential skills for success in the 21st century.

🎨

Arts

Our Arts program offers a range of courses in music, art, drama, and dance, providing students with a platform to express themselves creatively. Our experienced teachers encourage students to explore their imagination and develop their artistic skills.

🏀

Athletics

Our Athletics program offers a range of sports and physical activities, promoting teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness. Our experienced coaches and trainers help students develop essential skills for success on and off the field.
