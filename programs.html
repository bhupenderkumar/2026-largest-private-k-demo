Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for children aged 3-4, focusing on social, emotional, and cognitive development. Our experienced teachers create a nurturing environment that fosters a love of learning and exploration.

📚

Elementary

In our Elementary program, students in grades K-4 engage in a rigorous and engaging curriculum that promotes critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration. Our small class sizes ensure personalized attention and support.

🤓

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper programs for grades 5-8 offer a challenging and supportive learning environment, where students develop their academic skills, explore their interests, and build lasting relationships with their peers and teachers.

💻

STEM

Our STEM program integrates science, technology, engineering, and math to inspire curiosity and innovation in our students. We provide hands-on experiences, real-world applications, and cutting-edge tools to prepare our students for a rapidly changing world.

🎨

Arts

At Largest Private K, we believe that arts education is essential to a well-rounded education. Our Arts program offers a variety of courses and extracurricular activities, from music and visual arts to drama and dance.

🏋️‍♀️

Athletics

Our Athletics program fosters teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness in our students. We offer a range of sports teams and activities, from basketball and soccer to cross-country and volleyball.
