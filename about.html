Our Mission & History

Largest Private K has been a staple in the Houston community since 1988, proudly serving families with a values-driven education. Our school's rich history is built on a foundation of academic rigor, social responsibility, and emotional intelligence.

Our teaching approach is centered around project-based learning, encouraging students to explore their passions and develop a growth mindset. Our experienced faculty tailor their instruction to meet the unique needs of each student.

Beyond the classroom, our students engage in a wide range of extracurricular activities, from sports and clubs to community service projects. We nurture our students' creativity, physical health, and emotional well-being to help them become well-rounded individuals.
