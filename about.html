Our Mission & History

Founded in 2010, 2026 Largest Private K has established itself as a premier private school in Austin, Texas, committed to providing a high-quality education that prepares our students for success in an ever-changing world.

Our teaching approach emphasizes hands-on learning experiences, project-based education, and personalized attention to ensure that each child receives the support and guidance they need to thrive.

From art clubs and sports teams to music programs and community service initiatives, we offer a wide range of extracurricular activities that help our students develop important life skills, build lasting relationships, and become responsible members of society.
